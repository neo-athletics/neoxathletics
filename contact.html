About

My name is Juan Mario Rodriguez, I am here to help you reach your fitness goals at best of my ability and knowledge. I have worked in the fitness industry for about 3 years now. Started as a personal trainer at YMCA. From there I transition to EBC and started as a floor trainer. I been a part of the fitness community since I was 18 years old. Even after many years in the fitness industry, I still strive in my endeavor of new and challenging fitness goals. In my journey I have helped friends and family reach their goals. I am here today to present to you neoxathletics. I have created this website with content to help those who are new into the fitness community or just trying to get a refresh on some fitness tips. Hope this helps you guys in some way.
